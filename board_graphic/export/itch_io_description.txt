<p>&nbsp;ChessIto has a few minor differences from traditional chess :</p>
<ul>
   <li>&nbsp;No stalemate or draw. If you can't move, you lose.</li>
   <li>&nbsp;Promotion is forced to Queen</li>
   <li>&nbsp;No repetition rule</li>
</ul>
